Workato

Workato is an integration automation SAAS company. Through their platform, users can automate workflows using various apps by building ‘recipes’. A recipe is essentially a set of tasks that will execute when a trigger event occurs. My job as an intern was to integrate AWS Simple Notification Service (SNS) into the platform, thus allowing users to include SNS tasks in their recipes. As Workato’s code base is primarily built on Rails, I did all of my work in Ruby. As a bonus with this project, I was able to learn a great deal about AWS as well through having to navigate its documentation while working on the connector.

Avi Networks

Avi Networks is a multi-cloud application delivery company that was acquired by VMware in 2019. My job as an intern there was to integrate their testing libraries when they made the switch from Red Hat OpenShift to Google’s Kubernetes as their containerization software. While I wasn’t quite developing software the way I did at Workato the next year, I still learned a great deal by having to interact with Kubernetes on a daily basis and go through its documentation to implement functions of my own.

Bruin Sports Analytics


Bruin Sports Analytics is UCLA’s sports data science club. It was created during my first year of college and I’ve been with it ever since. I currently serve as the journalism chair, managing the quarterly articles that are written for our website. I also currently run the website itself (built on Jekyll framework), adding and removing content as needed. Sports analytics has become one of my favorite hobbies over the past few years and I have written quite a few articles of my own for the club.

BlackBox Trading

BlackBox Trading is a project that I’ve been working on with some friends for over a year now. Our team creates systematic trading strategies based on statistical factors. I do the actual development of the strategies, using python and the Quantopian API. After thoroughly backtesting our strategies over various time periods and stress events, we deploy them through a cloud PAAS. I didn’t have any knowledge of trading on the stock market before joining this project so it has been a very interesting endeavor for me and likely something I do for a long time to come.

Eagle Scout Project

Eagle Scout is the highest attainable rank in Boy Scouting. The Eagle Project is something that all Eagle candidates must complete in order to become an Eagle Scout. The project generally encompasses heavy planning and leadership, as the candidate must lead a group of people to complete the project rather than simply do it on his own. For my project, I built a 30 foot retaining wall for a trail at Tule Ponds, an outdoor educational facility in my hometown of Fremont. The months of planning paid off, as we managed to successfully complete the entire project in one day.
